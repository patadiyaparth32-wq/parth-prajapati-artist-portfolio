# Future Improvements

Recommended features for future iterations of the website:

- **Headless CMS Integration:**
  Connect the site to Sanity.io or Strapi so the artist can upload new drawings and modify text without editing JSON files.
  
- **Online Store (E-Commerce):**
  Integrate Stripe or Shopify Lite to sell original artwork, prints, and drawing materials.
  
- **Interactive Booking Calendar:**
  Integrate Calendly or Cal.com so students can schedule drawing mentorship sessions directly.
  
- **Blog Section:**
  Add a blog for sharing drawing tutorials, materials reviews, and art news to boost organic SEO.
